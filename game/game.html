Attack On Teemo



Teemo

Summary:
Use Teemo to destroy everything!
Gameplay:
Use “wasd” as up, left, down, and right, or arrows to control Teemo and mice as aim to shoot darts at the enemy. You can also gain new skills when you get to a higher level. Kill all enemies or Boss to win to game.

Enemies:


(from left to right: Minion, Elite minion, ASH)
Main enemy weaklings that deal melee damage to Teemo


(from left to right: Cannon, Elite cannon, GHOST)
Elite enemies that shoot black cannon balls to Teemo


Azure Dragon: Final boss that shoot gray spikes and blow fire to Teemo
